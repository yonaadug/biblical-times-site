Privacy Policy

Last updated · 2026-06-16

Biblical Times is a weekly devotional published by Biblical Times LLC. We try to keep the privacy story as simple as the reading itself.

What we collect

From the app: nothing personal. There is no sign-in, no account, and no profile. We do not collect names, email addresses, advertising identifiers, location, or device identifiers. Your reading streak, marked-complete state, bookmarks, and preferences are stored only on your device, in the app’s local storage.

From the website (when you support us financially in the future): donations will be processed by Stripe. Stripe collects whatever it needs to process the payment (card details, billing email, country), per their privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy. Biblical Times LLC would only see the donation amount, the date, and the email you give Stripe — so we can send a receipt and (with your consent) a thank-you note. We do not sell, share, or trade that email with anyone.

What we don’t do

  • We do not run ads.
  • We do not track you across sites or apps.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs.
  • We do not have a marketing CRM with your data in it.
  • We do not store your card details (Stripe does, not us).

Children

Biblical Times is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Your rights

Because we collect essentially no personal data from the app, there is very little to exercise rights over. If you’d like a copy of any donation records we have on you, or want them deleted, email us. We will respond within a few days.

Changes

If we ever change this policy, the new version will live at this URL with the date above updated. Material changes will be flagged in the app.
